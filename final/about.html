Our Story

Pacific Trails Resort was created to craft unforgettable experiences. Nestled in Zephyr, California, we invite you to unplug, explore, and feel at home in nature. From sunrise hikes to cozy yurts under the stars, every detail is designed with care from locally sourced meals to eco friendly design using sustainable materials, energy efficient systems, and low impact practices. Our commitment to protecting the environment ensures that the landscapes we love remain vibrant and thriving, so you can enjoy adventures while leaving a positive footprint and creating memories that last a lifetime.

Meet Our Team

Our team is made up of outdoor lovers, hospitality pros, and adventure guides all rolled into one. Every member is passionate about nature and dedicated to making your stay unforgettable. Whether we are leading a sunrise hike, sharing insider trail tips, or whipping up a farm to table breakfast, we make every moment feel personal, effortless, and full of memories. We also believe in the power of community. Our team grows stronger together, whether it is during a beach retreat, planning experiences for guests, or supporting each other behind the scenes.
